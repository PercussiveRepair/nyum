To make 200ml 
* `10g` sugar
* `1/2 tsp` cocoa powder (optional)
* `160ml` cold milk
* `40ml` hot water 
* `5g` good instant coffee
 
> Mix coffee, sugar and hot water in a jug until dissolved
>
> Add cocoa powder if desired
>
> Mix in cold milk and chill for several hours
