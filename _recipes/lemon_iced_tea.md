* `2-3` tea bags
* `1½l` boiling water
* `⅓ -½ cup` sugar
* `1` lemon sliced
* `¼ cup` lemon juice
 
> Put tea and boiling water into a heatproof jug or saucepan and let steep for 5-10 mins
>
> Mix in sugar til dissolved.
>
> Mix in lemon juice and lemon
>
> Top up with 1 ltr cold water and store in the fridge
