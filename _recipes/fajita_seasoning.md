title: Fajita seasoning 
category: Sauces & Seasonings
veggie: y

--- 
* `1 tbsp` cornstarch
* `2 tsp` chilli powder
* `1 tsp` salt
* `1 tsp` paprika
* `1 tsp` sugar
* `¾ tsp` crushed chicken bouillon cube
* `½ tsp` onion powder
* `¼ tsp` garlic powder
* `¼ tsp` cayenne pepper
* `¼ tsp` cumin
 
>
> Mix and store in an airtight container
