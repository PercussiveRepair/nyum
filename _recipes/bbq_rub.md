* `¼ cup` dark brown sugar
* `¼ cup` paprika
* `2 tbsp` cracked black pepper
* `2 tbsp` sea salt
* `1 tbsp` lemon pepper
* `1 tbsp` celery salt
* `1 tbsp` garlic powder
* `2 tsp` chili powder
* `1 tsp` mustard powder
* `1 tsp` cumin powder
* `1 tsp` dried oregano


> Mix all and keep in a airtight jar for up to 3 months
